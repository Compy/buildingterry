Invest in Terry's Future

Terry has launched its most ambitious capital campaign with a goal of raising $90 million in private funding. The Building Terry campaign builds on an already strong foundation that will take the college from great to excellent.

We seek to enhance the student experience by:

  • Building Opportunity: New Business Learning Community — $70,000,000
  • Building Knowledge: Recruit, retain, support top faculty — $10,000,000
  • Building Value: Strengthen academic programs — $10,000,000

We are counting on the generosity of Terry alumni and friends to join forces with our exemplary faculty and students to create a new standard for business education.

Daniel P. Amos (BBA ’73) Chair, Building Terry Campaign
